Welcome to AVM LABS

AVM LABS PVT LTD (Formerly Known as BEST STANDARDS TECHNOLOGIES PVT LTD) is an accredited calibration lab under the group of AVM.


AVM LABS, focused on providing reliable and quality assured Instrumentation services.


AVM LABS offers the most comprehensive of calibration services such as Temperature, Electro-Technical, Mechanical (Dimensional, Pressure, Mass, Force, Torque, Volume, Density, Air Flow & Liquid Flow) & many more. Calibrations are done both at the Laboratory and on-site (Customer's Premises).


AVM LABS has a ‘state of the art’, calibration laboratory which provides quality calibration and repairs for pressure and temperature instrumentation.


Through extensive in-house training and on site experience, our team at AVM LABS has the skills, qualifications and innovative and practical solutions for your business requirements. Employing world recognized test practices and methodology; we remain market leaders in the Calibration industry.


At AVM LABS we understand that Customers needs fast, efficient service and that means equipment must be calibrated and back in operation as quickly as possible. That’s why we endeavor to have all industrial grade equipment turned around within 24 hours, with 48 hours required for test grade equipment requiring greater accuracy. However in cases of extreme urgency we will work with the client to complete the calibration within the required time frame.


Ensuring that instruments are calibrated accurately requires a high level of expertise; at AVM LABS, we pride ourselves on ensuring that every job is carried out to meet stringent Indian & International standards.
